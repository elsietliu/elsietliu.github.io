Experience

Novartis

May 2022 - August 2022

Last summer, I interned at Novartis in Cambridge, MA as a software developer. I worked on their Scientific Registration Product, which provides persistent data storage and querying of scientific business object instances, such as Studies, Samples, Subjects, Measurements, etc. Specifically, I helped to develop a "Default" Source of Truth. It's main features that I helped implement are:

  • database as a service (REST Data API)
  • schema-less document-oriented storage
  • transactional, ACID semantics and batch storage
  • consistent read after write
  • immutable history of instance states
  • temporal versioning and lookups

Technologies: Java, Spring Boot, Maven, Jira, Git (Bitbucket), Jenkins, Docker, Citus (Postgres SQL), Stored Procedures, pgAdmin, JDBC, MS Teams, Windows, deployed to AWS ECS

Researcher for Intelligent Screenreaders

May 2021 - August 2021

I worked as a research assistant for Professor Qian Yang's lab through Cornell's Computer Science Undergraduate Research Program (CSURP). I participated in literature reviews regarding current screenreaders and compared the order in which they read web content with a non-visually-impaired individual's inherent perception of organization.
